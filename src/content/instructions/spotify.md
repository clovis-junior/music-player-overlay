You need to create an app in your Spotify dashboard. [Click here to create your app on Spotify](https://developer.spotify.com/dashboard).

**Follow the instructions:**

- Click **Create App**
- Fill in the following details:
    - **Redirect URIs**: `{url}`
    - **Which API/SDKs are you planning to use?** — check Web API
    - The `App name` and `App description` can be whatever you want.
- Click **Settings**.
    - Click `View client secret`
    - Copy your `Client ID` and `Client Secret`
- Fill in your `Client ID` and `Client Secret`.
- Click the **Authenticate** button below.
